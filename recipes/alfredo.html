Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients

Sauce

  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Chicken

  • Chicken breasts
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Shrimp

  • Shrimp
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Paprika
  • Chili flakes
  • White pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

Sauce

  1. Melt butter in a pan and add seasonings.
  2. Add heavy cream and let simmer on low for ~2 minutes.
  3. Add parmesan and stir until melted.

Chicken

  1. Slice breasts into cutlets.
  2. Season cutlets.
  3. Make an egg wash.
  4. Season breadcrumbs.
  5. Double coat each cutlet with eggs and breadcrumbs.
  6. Pre-heat air-fryer to 400 degree Fahrenheit.
  7. Place cutlets in the air-fryer for 15 minutes.
  8. Flip cutlets at 7 minutes and spray oil.

Shrimp

  1. Oil shrimp.
  2. Add minced garlic and seasonings.
  3. Add lemon zest and juice.
  4. Cook with oil in a pan over medium-heat until golden brown.

Assembly

  1. Cook fettucine according to package instructions.
  2. Mix in fettucine into the alfredo sauce.
  3. Add extra parmesan on top.
  4. Additional toppings:
    • Sauteed mushrooms
    • Sauteed asparagus
    • Sauteed broccoli
