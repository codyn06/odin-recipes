Lasagna

Description

Classic lasagna with ricotto, mozzerella, parmesan and ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 container ricotta cheese
  • 1 bag mozzerella cheese
  • 1 bag parmesan cheese
  • Ready-to-bake lasagna noodles
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef on high heat until brown. Drain excess fat when finished.
  3. Add marinara sauce with seasonings to the skillet with ground beef and let simmer.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the container of ricotta cheese with half of the bag of mozzerella and half of the bag of parmesan cheese. Add salt.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Cover bottom with a layer of lasagna noodles, then cheese mixture, then meat sauce. Repeat until out of meat sauce.
  7. Top the dish with mozzerella and parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes or recommended time on the lasagna noodles box.
